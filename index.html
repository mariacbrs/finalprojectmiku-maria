History of cinematic industry

The Cinematic industry started in the early 19th century. At the beginning, movies were very short with only a few minutes. They were usually shown at a music hall. In 1914, Europe, Russia and Scandinavia had very strong industries. The American industry became popular after the first WW when Europe got affected. In Canada, the cinematic industry started in Montreal in 1896. 2 years later, Vancouver had the first screening.
