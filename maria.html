MOVIES

As said in the introduction of the page the first movie was shown in December 28 of 1895 in "Grand Café" (Paris, France), the machine used was the Cinématographe created by the Lumière brothers, considered the pioneers of the industry. Since the antiquity humans try to make moving images, but in the 17th century a machine called magic flashlight was created, the first machine created, it could project draws made in glass through lenses and got very famous at the time, being use by many people in different areas of knowledge.

OSCARS

Known worldwide, the Oscars is considered the most famous award in the cinematographic world, watched in more than 200 countries.Held at the beginning of the year, the first award was in 1929 with 12 categories, nowadays are 24 categories and since 2002 are realized in Hollywood. The statue is made of gold-plated but in the beggining were made in bronze with 24-karat golden. Results of the first Oscars:

  • ACTOR: EMIL JANNINGS
  • ACTRESS: JANET GAYNOR
  • ART DIRECTION: THE DOVE
  • CINEMATOGRAPHY: SUNRISE
  • COMEDY PICTURE: TWO ARABIAN KNIGHTS
  • FRAMATIC PICTURE: 7TH HEAVEN
  • ENGINEERING EFECTS: WINGS
  • OUTSTANDING PICTURE: WINGS
  • SPECIAL AWARD: THE CIRCUS
  • UNIQUE AND ARTISTIC PICTURE: SUNRISE
  • ADAPTATION: 7TH HEAVEN
  • ORIGINAL STORY: UNDERWORLD
  • TITLE WRITING: JOSEPH FARNHAM

ACTORS

The most important role in the cinematic universe is the actor/actress, without the cast there's only a screen with background. All the magic of the cinema depends on these people and the emotion they shown on the screen.

The actors have the ability to become one with the character and through this make the viewer find similarities, feel fear, happiness, sadness, and other emotions with expressions, body language, and so on.
